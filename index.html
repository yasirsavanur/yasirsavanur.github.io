Visualising Indians & Chinese Immigration to Canada

Explore the migration patterns of Indians and Chinese to Canada from 1980 to 2013 through captivating visualisations. This project utilizes Matplotlib to create diverse plots such as line plots, histograms, bar charts, box plots, bubble plots, waffle charts, regression plots, and maps. Gain valuable insights and hands-on experience in data visualization while uncovering trends and patterns in migration dynamics.

Data Analysis of Diabetes patients of Pima Indians

Explore data analysis of diabetes patients from the Pima Indian heritage to predict diabetes occurrence. With a broader perspective, analyze the dataset through the lens of a dietitian, bridging the gap between datasets and human knowledge.

EDA of Automobile Data from the 1985 Ward's Automotive Yearbook

This repository showcases the EDA of automobile data from the 1985 Ward's Automotive Yearbook. Using Python libraries and EDA techniques, we explore fuel consumption rates, factors influencing car prices, trade-in evaluations, and customer appraisals. The project highlights utilisation of Matplotlib in data analysis, and car purchasing insights.

Bangalore Accidents Geo-Spatial Analysis

Explore the patterns and trends of Bangalore accidents using geo-spatial analysis. This Tableau project utilises data from Collision Avoidance Systems in BMTC buses to identify factors contributing to accidents, evaluate warning system effectiveness, and provide targeted safety recommendations. Gain insights from interactive visualizations on Tableau Public.

Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2019 - Technology Trends and Analysis

Gain valuable insights into technology trends and future preferences of developers worldwide through interactive dashboards created using Tableau. Explore top programming languages, databases, platforms, and web frameworks, as well as demographics based on gender, countries, age, and education level. This project showcases data visualisation and analysis skills on Tableau Public, providing a comprehensive view of the Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2019.

Prediction of House Sales in King County, USA

This project utilizes Python-DA techniques to predict house sales in King County, USA, providing valuable insights into the housing market. Through regression models and interactive dashboards created with Tableau, it offers a comprehensive analysis of factors influencing housing prices, empowering users to make informed decisions.
